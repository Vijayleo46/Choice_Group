The Choice School is ranked as one of the leading schools in India and the best in Kerala. It has been the preferred choice for parents across the world. The school maintains a holistic balance of curricular and extra-curricular activities that unleashes the potential of every child and encourages students to be the best version of themselves.

Online registration for admission for the new academic year commences in August of each year and is the first step in the procedure.

What makes us the best

In our endeavour to mould the best teachers for the coming generation of school students, we are in search of passionate youngsters from across the globe to join YETP.
Founded on the principles of Knowledge, Character and Health, Choice School focuses on all round development through academic excellence and extra-curricular activities.
State-of-the-art tech and digital solutions for better learning outcomes from Kindergarten to Grade 12.
To bring out the best in each and every student and empower them to unleash their full potential.
A happy, caring and stimulating environment that stresses on honesty, integrity and cultural awareness.
